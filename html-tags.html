  • hr tag - To make horizontal line. This is a self closing tag.
  • b tag - To give boldness to text. Any text given in it appears to be bold
  • i tag - To give a slanding style to text. Any text given in it appears to be in italics
  • u tag - To underline a text. Any text given in it appears to be underlined
  • br tag - To skip to next line. Another self closing tag.
